Carolina Preserve

An active-adult community with a 35,000-square-foot clubhouse, walking trails, and a robust calendar of resident clubs.

Set on 1,300 wooded acres just minutes from Cary’s town center, Carolina Preserve is one of the Triangle’s most established 55+ communities. The clubhouse anchors social life, but the resident-run clubs are the heart of the place — there are over 90 of them, from pickleball to woodworking to investment book club.

Amenities

  • 35,000-sqft clubhouse
  • Indoor & outdoor pools
  • Tennis & pickleball courts
  • Fitness center with classes
  • Walking & biking trails
  • Resident-run gardening club
