Our Favorite Neighborhoods

Five Lowcountry pockets our agents quietly send their best clients to. All five share one zip code and a single county courthouse.

South of Broad

Antebellum homes, garden walls draped in jasmine, and one of the most photographed streetscapes in America. Walking distance to the Battery and the marina.

Harleston Village

The college’s residential heart. Smaller-than-Broad homes with the same charm and a livelier pulse on weekend evenings.

Cannonborough-Elliotborough

Where the long-time locals still rent, the renovations are happening fast, and the best coffee is on Spring Street.

Mazyck-Wraggborough

Quietly residential, lined with single-family Charlestonians on deep lots. Walking distance to upper King.

Radcliffeborough

The hidden gem — pre-war duplexes that have been brought back, with porches on every floor and live oaks shading the street.

Why Charleston, SC?

Because the people. Because the food. Because the marsh. We’d list more reasons but our office is in the city and we’d like to keep our parking spaces.
