Pinnacle Peak Estates

A guard-gated north-valley enclave of custom homes on view lots.

Tucked against the base of one of the most photographed mountains in the state, Pinnacle Peak Estates offers oversized, one-of-a-kind homesites with unobstructed mountain and sunset views. Architectural diversity is part of the charm — territorial, contemporary, and Tuscan villas all coexist here.
