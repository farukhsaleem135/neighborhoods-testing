Bedford Village Estates

A small cluster of New England Colonials tucked off a country road, on a fourteen-acre wooded parcel.

Twelve carefully sited homes share a private cul-de-sac, a stone-walled common, and views of the protected forest beyond. Builders here favored authentic detail — hand-planed floors, double-hung windows, and copper gutters — and most homes were sold pre-completion to local buyers who knew exactly what they wanted.

Architectural details

  • Center-chimney Colonial floor plans
  • Wide-plank hardwood throughout
  • Authentic period millwork
  • Custom-built fireplaces

The setting

Country road, town-maintained but lightly trafficked. Two miles to the village green. Eight miles to the airport. New England weather, with all four seasons clearly distinguishable.

Availability

Resale only at this point. New listings come once or twice a year — we recommend joining our list to be notified first.
